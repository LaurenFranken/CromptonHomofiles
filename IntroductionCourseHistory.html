October 27, 1992

303 Andrews 0333

Joseph Svoboda

University Archives

308 Love Library 0410

Dear Joe,

I repeatedly get requests for information about the course on homosexuality that caused controversy back in 1970.

I thought it might be useful for students and future historians of the university if I wrote out a personal statement and included some press references to be added to your present file on the subject.

Sincerely,

Louis Crompton
